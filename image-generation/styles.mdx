A Style is a curated set of visual parameters that shifts the overall aesthetic of the output — color grading, texture, lighting mood — without changing the model or the prompt.

## How to apply a Style

1. Open the generator at [pornx.co/create](https://pornx.co/create)
2. Select a Style from the Styles panel
3. Generate as normal — the Style applies on top of your model and prompt

Only one Style can be active at a time. Styles stack with LoRAs and Actions.

## Tips

- If the Style is overpowering your prompt, simplify the prompt and let the Style carry the aesthetic
- Styles work best when the model and Style are aligned — a cinematic Style on the Cinematic model produces more consistent results than on Cartoon
- Switch Styles without changing your prompt to quickly compare aesthetics on the same composition
