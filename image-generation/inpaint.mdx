InPaint lets you select a region of an existing image and redraw it using a new prompt. The rest of the image stays intact.

## How to use InPaint

1. Open any image you've generated
2. Click **InPaint**
3. Paint over the area you want to change — use a brush size that covers the region cleanly
4. Write a prompt describing what you want in that area
5. Click **Generate** — only the masked region is redrawn

## Tips

- Keep the InPaint prompt focused on the selected area only — describing the full image confuses the output
- Use a slightly larger mask than the area you want to change — tight masks produce hard edges
- If the result doesn't blend well, run InPaint again with a larger mask that overlaps the surrounding area
- InPaint works on any generated image regardless of which model produced it

## Common issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The redrawn area doesn't match the rest of the image">
    The mask edge is too tight. Expand the mask to overlap slightly with the surrounding area and regenerate.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The result ignores my prompt">
    Prompt Enhancer may be overriding your input. Toggle it off in the settings panel and try again with a more specific prompt.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="InPaint is not available on my image">
    InPaint is only available on images generated within your current account. Gallery images must be saved to your account first.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
