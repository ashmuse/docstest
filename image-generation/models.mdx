Each model produces a distinct look. The prompt is the same — the model decides how it's interpreted.

## Realism

Photorealistic output with accurate skin texture, natural lighting, and true-to-life proportions.

**Pick this when:**

- You want the result to look like a photograph
- Skin texture, hair detail, and lighting accuracy matter
- You want photographic quality in the first frame before animating

**Avoid when:** you want a stylized, illustrated, or rendered look — use Anime, 3D, or Cartoon instead.

## Anime

Clean 2D illustration style with bold linework, flat shading, and expressive character design.

**Pick this when:**

- You want anime or manga-style output
- You're generating characters with stylized proportions or exaggerated features
- You want a recognizable illustrated aesthetic

**Avoid when:** you want photorealism or a 3D-rendered look — use Realism or 3D instead.

{/* TODO: screenshot after /create redesign ships — should show an Anime output, female character, stylized face */}

**Example prompt:** `anime girl, long silver hair, school uniform, cherry blossom background, detailed linework, soft shading`

---

## Cinematic

Film-grade output with dramatic lighting, strong color grading, and a high-production aesthetic.

**Pick this when:**

- You want the result to look like a still from a film or editorial shoot
- Mood, contrast, and color temperature are important
- You're generating scenes with strong directional light

**Avoid when:** you want clean natural light — use Realism instead.

{/* TODO: screenshot after /create redesign ships — should show a Cinematic output, dramatic lighting, strong shadows */}

**Example prompt:** `a woman in a red dress, standing in a dark corridor, single overhead light, cinematic color grade, film still`

---

## 3D

Stylized 3D-rendered characters with soft volumetric shading, smooth surfaces, and a game-art or CGI aesthetic.

**Pick this when:**

- You want a polished rendered look rather than photographic or flat illustration
- You're generating characters with stylized but three-dimensional presence
- You want something between realism and cartoon

**Avoid when:** you want flat 2D illustration — use Anime or Cartoon instead.

{/* TODO: screenshot after /create redesign ships — should show a 3D output, rendered female character, soft lighting */}

**Example prompt:** `3D rendered woman, short blonde hair, futuristic outfit, studio lighting, smooth skin, high detail, CGI style`

---

## Cartoon

Flat graphic style suited to pin-up, comic, and retro illustration aesthetics.

**Pick this when:**

- You want a flat, graphic, or illustrative result
- You're going for a pin-up, comic-book, or retro poster look
- Bold outlines and simplified shading are part of the aesthetic

**Avoid when:** you want detailed illustration with anime-style linework — use Anime instead.

{/* TODO: screenshot after /create redesign ships — should show a Cartoon output, pin-up style, flat colors */}

**Example prompt:** `retro pin-up illustration, woman in a swimsuit, bold outlines, flat colors, 1950s style, graphic poster aesthetic`

---

## Versatile / Studio

A general-purpose model that handles mixed styles and experimental prompts without committing to a single aesthetic.

**Pick this when:**

- You're exploring and don't want to commit to a specific style
- Your prompt mixes styles or is hard to categorize
- You want to compare outputs across models before committing

**Avoid when:** you have a clear target aesthetic — a specialized model will get you there faster.

{/* TODO: screenshot after /create redesign ships — should show a Versatile output */}

**Example prompt:** `a woman in a fantasy forest, soft light, detailed environment, mixed painterly and photographic style`
