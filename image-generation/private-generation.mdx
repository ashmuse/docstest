Private Generation keeps your output off the public gallery and out of any shared feed. No one else sees what you generate — not other users, not the community gallery.

## How to enable it

1. Open the generator at [pornx.co/create](https://pornx.co/create)
2. Toggle **Private** before generating
3. Generate as normal — all results from that session are private

Private mode stays on until you turn it off. Switch it per session depending on whether you want to publish or keep results to yourself.

## What private means

- Your generations do not appear in the public gallery
- Other users cannot see, remix, or interact with your images
- Your images are still accessible to you in your personal gallery
- Private generation uses tokens — see [/premium](/premium) for current rates

## Tips

- Enable Private by default if you're working on something personal or experimental
- You can publish a private image later by changing its visibility in your gallery
- Private Generation applies per generation — switching it off mid-session makes subsequent results public
