## **How to use:**

1. Open the [/Create](https://pornx.co/create) page
2. Choose the **Model**
3. Choose the filters you need or write your **Prompt**
4. Click on **Actions**
5. Select any Action you like (You can add to favorites by clicking on the heart icon)
6. Click **Generate**

<Note>
  \*\*Be careful with filters! \*\*You should avoid conflicting filters; they can break your entire scene. **E.g.,** when choosing **Doggy anal**, you should not select breast filters, as they will not be displayed in the scene, but they will start to conflict and may show broken images.
</Note>
