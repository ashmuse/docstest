**Prompt feature** - allows you to create any image based on the text you write.\
How to write the best prompts, you can find in this instruction: [**Instruction**](https://docs.pornx.co/getting-started/create-your-image/writing-prompt)

## <mark style="color:green;">**How to use:**</mark>

1. Open the [/Create](https://pornx.co/create) page
2. Choose the [Model](https://docs.pornx.co/getting-started/create-your-image/models)
3. Click on the **PEN ICON**
4. Write your Positive prompt and Negative prompt
5. Click on **Generate**
