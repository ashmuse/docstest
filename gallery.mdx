The Gallery is a continuously updated feed of community-published generations. It's one of the main ways to discover what's possible on PornX, find prompts and styles that work, and start new generations from existing images and videos.

## Browsing and discovery

The Gallery opens on a personalized feed. Scroll to load more content. Use the search bar to find specific styles, subjects, or keywords.

Every image and video in the Gallery shows the model used to generate it. Click any image to open the full view with generation details.

## How the recommendation algorithm works

The Gallery feed is personalized. The more you use PornX — liking images, saving to collections, generating content — the more the feed reflects your taste.

The algorithm surfaces content based on:

- Images you've liked and saved
- Models and styles you generate with most
- Categories you spend time in

It updates continuously. A new account sees a general feed; after a session or two of activity the feed starts to narrow toward your preferences.

The algorithm does not surface content at random. If you want to explore outside your usual feed, browse by category directly or use search.

## Using a Gallery image as a starting point

Any Gallery image can be used to start a new generation.

**Remix** — opens the image in the generator with the original model and prompt pre-filled. Edit the prompt or swap the model and generate a variation.

**Image-to-Video** — sends the image directly to the video generator as a first frame. Only works cleanly if the source image was generated with Realism. See [Image-to-Video](/video-generation/image-to-video).

**Videos** — community-published videos play inline. You cannot remix a video directly, but you can open the source image if the creator published it alongside the clip.

To access these options, open the full image view and click **Remix** or **Animate**.

## Publishing your own generations

Generations are public by default — they appear in the community Gallery as soon as they're created. Publication is permanent. If you don't want a generation to appear in the Gallery, enable Private Generation before generating. See [Private Generation](/image-generation/private-generation).

Published content must follow the [Community Guidelines](/community-guidelines).

**What gets published with the image or video:**

- The user id and name
- The model used
- The prompt, unless you generated with Private Generation enabled

Private generations cannot be published directly. Generate without Private mode if you intend to share the result.

Published content must follow the [Community Guidelines](/community-guidelines).

## Tips

- Use Remix on Gallery images to reverse-engineer prompts and styles that work
- Save images you want to animate before leaving the Gallery — finding them again in a large feed takes time
- Like content actively in your first few sessions to train the recommendation feed faster
- Browse by category when the main feed starts to feel repetitive
