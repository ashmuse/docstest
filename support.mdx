For any issue not covered in the docs, contact the support team directly.

## Contact

**Email:** [support@pornx.co](mailto:support@pornx.co)

**Discord:** [discord.gg/wE8z47gbhF](https://discord.gg/wE8z47gbhF) — fastest response for general questions and community help.

## Before contacting support

Check the troubleshooting pages first — most common issues are covered there:

- [Generation Issues](/troubleshooting/generation)
- [Payment Issues](/troubleshooting/payment)

## What to include

The faster you give context, the faster the issue gets resolved.

**For generation issues:** approximate time of the failed generation, model used, and whether tokens were deducted.

**For payment issues:** transaction ID or hash, payment method, and amount.

**For account issues:** the email address on your account.
