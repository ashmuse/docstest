title: Generating with Characters
description: How to create images featuring your saved characters.
---

Once you have a character saved, you can use them in any image generation session. The model applies their appearance automatically — your prompt covers everything else.

## How to generate

Open the [Image Generator](https://pornx.co/create). In the Characters panel, select the character you want to use — they'll be highlighted with an orange border. Then write your prompt, pick a resolution, and tap **Generate**.

Each generation costs 2 tokens.

## What to put in the prompt

With a character selected, describe the scene or action only. The character's appearance is already defined.

*✓ sitting by a window, rainy day, cozy interior*
*✗ a woman with orange hair sitting by a window*

Describing the character again can confuse the model and produce inconsistent results.

## Resolution

Character generations support all standard resolutions. For portraits, **Tall Portrait (7:9)** tends to work best. For full-body shots, try **Short Portrait (9:7)** or **Extra Tall (4:7)**.

See [Parameters](/image-generation/parameters) for the full resolution reference.

## Tips

- Keep prompts focused on lighting, setting, and action — the character handles the rest.
- If the result drifts from the character's appearance, try a shorter prompt.
- Use **Show all** to browse all saved characters if you have more than two.
