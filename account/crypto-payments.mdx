PornX accepts cryptocurrency via [NOWPayments](https://nowpayments.io/). The recommended currency is **USDT (TRC-20)**.

<Warning>Send a small amount extra to cover the network fee — underpayments are not automatically credited.</Warning>

## What you need

- An account on a crypto exchange or a wallet with USDT (TRC-20)

If you don't have one yet, these exchanges are straightforward to set up:

- [Binance](https://binance.com) — largest global exchange
- [ByBit](https://bybit.com)
- [Huobi](https://huobi.com) — popular in Asian markets
- [Coinbase](https://coinbase.com)

## How to pay

1. Go to [/premium](/premium) and select a plan or token package
2. Choose **Cryptocurrency** at checkout
3. Select **USDT TRC-20** from the dropdown and click **Proceed to payment**
4. Copy the wallet address shown on the NOWPayments page
5. Open your exchange or wallet, find USDT, and click **Withdraw**
6. Paste the wallet address
7. Select **TRC-20** as the network
8. Enter the exact amount shown — or slightly more to cover the fee
9. Click **Withdraw** to send

Premium or tokens activate once the transaction confirms on-chain.

<Note>Always select TRC-20 as the network. Sending on the wrong network results in a lost transaction.</Note>

## Confirmation time

Most transactions confirm within a few minutes. During high network congestion it can take up to thirty minutes.

## Common issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I sent the payment but my account hasn't updated">
    Wait for on-chain confirmation — up to thirty minutes during congestion. If your account still hasn't updated after that, contact [support](mailto:support@pornx.co) with your transaction hash.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I sent the wrong amount">
    Contact [support](mailto:support@pornx.co) with your transaction hash and the amount sent. Do not send a second payment before hearing back.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The payment address expired">
    Payment addresses expire after a set time. Go back to [/premium](/premium) and start a new checkout to get a fresh address.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I sent on the wrong network">
    Contact [support](mailto:support@pornx.co) immediately with your transaction hash. Recovery depends on the network used and is not guaranteed.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
