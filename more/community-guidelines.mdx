## What is prohibited

**Underage content**
Any content depicting characters that could be interpreted as under 18 is strictly prohibited. This is enforced automatically and manually.

**Non-consensual scenarios**
Content portraying non-consent, characters who are asleep, unconscious, intoxicated, or otherwise unable to consent is not allowed.

**Violence**
Content depicting violence, physical harm, coercion, or signs of harm — including handcuffs used in a coercive context, bruising, or scarring — is prohibited.

**Prohibited acts**
Coprophilia, bestiality, and zoophilia are not allowed.

**Real people**
All characters must be AI-generated. Content that resembles, mimics, or could be identified as a real living person — public figure or private individual — is prohibited.

**Illegal content**
Do not generate or share content that violates applicable laws or regulations.

**Harassment and hate speech**
Zero tolerance for harassment, bullying, or hate speech based on race, gender, sexual orientation, or any other characteristic.

**Impersonation and fraud**
Do not impersonate other users or engage in fraudulent activity.

**Service abuse**
Attempts to access paid features without payment, or to overload the generator in a way that degrades service for other users, will result in immediate restriction without refund.

## Watermarks

Generations may include a PornX watermark. Do not remove, modify, or obscure it. Do not edit generated content without explicit written permission from PornX.

## Reporting violations

If you see content that violates these guidelines, report it. We review all reports and take action where guidelines are breached.

## Consequences

Violations result in content removal, service restriction, or permanent account termination. PornX enforces these guidelines at its sole discretion.
